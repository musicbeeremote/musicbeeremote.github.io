# Playlists & Radio

## Playlists

<Row>
  <Phone src="/img/help/1.6/19_playlists.webp" alt="Playlists" />
</Row>

The **Playlists** screen (accessible from the drawer) supports folder navigation. Tap a folder to browse its contents, tap a playlist to start playing it. Each playlist has a play button for quick access.

Pull down to refresh the playlist list from MusicBee.

## Radio Stations

<Row>
  <Phone src="/img/help/1.6/20_radio.webp" alt="Radio stations" />
</Row>

The **Radio** screen (accessible from the drawer) lists your radio stations. They load automatically when you open the screen. A playing indicator shows which station is currently active. Tap a station or its play button to start playback.

Pull down to refresh the station list.
