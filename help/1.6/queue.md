# Queue

![Now Playing](/img/help/1.6/09_now_playing.webp)

The **Queue** screen (accessible from the drawer) shows MusicBee's active playing queue:

- **Playing indicator** — Highlights the currently playing track
- **Queue size** — Displayed in the header
- **Search** — Find a track in the queue
- **Swipe right** — Remove a track from the queue
- **Long-press and drag** — Reorder tracks
- **Pull down** — Refresh the queue

The queue refreshes automatically when you navigate to the screen.

## Track Actions

![Queue Context Menu](/img/help/1.6/28_queue_context_menu.webp)

Long-press a track in the queue to access its context menu:

- **Go to Album** — Navigate to the track's album in the library
- **Go to Artist** — Navigate to the track's artist in the library
