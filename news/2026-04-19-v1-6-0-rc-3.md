Two updates today: rc.3 is out with a handful of stability fixes, and the Play Store beta has been approved and is live on the testing channel.

---

## What changed in rc.3

Overnight testing on rc.2 surfaced a few crashes and stability issues that are now fixed in rc.3. If you're running rc.2, please update. The [rc.3 changelog](https://github.com/musicbeeremote/mbrc/releases/tag/v1.6.0-rc.3) has the full list of PRs.

No feature changes, no behavior changes, just fixes. The plugin you already have keeps working.

## Play Store beta is live

Google approved the permissions review, so the open beta is now available on the Play Store's testing channel. You can opt in and get updates through Play the same way you would any other app:

- Join the beta on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kelsos.mbrc).

GitHub Releases will keep getting the same builds as pre-releases, so if you prefer sideloading the APK that path isn't going anywhere.

## What's next

If the Play beta stays clean for a few days, v1.6.0 gets promoted to production and MusicBee Remote is back on the Play Store stable channel. If something breaks, another rc goes out first.

As always, crashes and weirdness go on [GitHub issues](https://github.com/musicbeeremote/mbrc/issues), and [Discord](https://discord.gg/rceTb57) is open for questions. Thanks to everyone who's been testing — the overnight reports are what made rc.3 possible.
